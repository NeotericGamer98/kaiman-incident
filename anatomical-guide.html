🔬 What is the "Small of the Back"?

Despite what the subject claims, the "small of the back" is a well-established anatomical region. It refers to the lumbar area of the spine, located between the bottom of the rib cage and the top of the pelvis.

Medical terminology: Lumbar region (L1–L5 vertebrae).
